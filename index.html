Filmmaker / Writer / Director

From the Yards

Short Film

Debut short film. A middle class family are forced to relocate to their old neighbourhood after the father loses his job due to a medical condition. Based on Anton Chekhov's short story 'Peasants'. Duration: 11 minutes 50 seconds.

Skimming Stones

Short Documentary

Short documentary piece. The film is about a widowed man in the Yorkshire Dales, looking at loss, home, and, ultimately, moving on. First year university submission. Shortlisted for Best Short Film at Hinterlands Film Festival 2019. Duration: 3 mins 1 second.

It Still Moves

Short Documentary

Short documentary piece. A family holiday is examined, exploring themes of the passing of time, familial dynamics, and video/photo as memory and document of the past. Duration: 14 minutes 31 seconds.
